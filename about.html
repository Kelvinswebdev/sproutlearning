About Sprout Learning

We are Sprout Learning — a home tuition initiative equipping Africa’s teens with future-ready skills, right at their doorstep.

We are Sprout Learning — a home tuition initiative equipping Africa’s teens with future-ready skills, right at their doorstep.


We believe in the power of creativity and hands-on learning to transform lives. By equipping teenagers with practical skills like web development, graphic design, and digital literacy, we unlock opportunities, inspire innovation, and empower the next generation of problem-solvers. Education is more than just textbooks—it’s about rethinking how young people learn, create, and contribute. Every young mind holds the potential to design, build, and shape a better future for themselves and their communities.

At Sprout Learning, we are committed to innovation in youth education, digital skills, and creative empowerment. Our work is about building a smarter, more skilled Africa by teaching real-life skills, mentoring teenagers, and inspiring the next generation to think, create, and lead. We partner with communities, parents, and local mentors to unlock the full potential of young minds across the continent.

Sprout Learning At A Glance

At Sprout Learning, our initiatives provide an inspiring glimpse into how we're empowering young people through tech, design, and real-life skills — all with a foundation in faith and sustainability.

Our Mission

To nurture and equip young people across Africa with practical, faith-driven skills in technology, design, and real-life disciplines — fostering purpose, creativity, and leadership in every community we serve.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading movement for empowering youth through Christ-centered creative education, shaping a future where every young person is equipped to build, serve, and lead with excellence.

Our Core Values

Innovation: We embrace creative solutions to recycling challenges.

Sustainability: We prioritize long-term environmental and economic impact.

Community: We work together to build healthier, empowered communities.

Empowerment Through Education We don’t just teach skills — we plant seeds of confidence, independence, and lifelong learning
