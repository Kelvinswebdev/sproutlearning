Abstract Submission

We invite academics, researchers, non-governmental organizations, community-based organizations, civil society groups, government ministries, departments, and agencies (MDAs), as well as development organizations to submit abstracts for the 1st African Recycling Initiative Conference in Accra. Submissions can be made via email or directly uploaded to the AFRICAReloop website.

Call for Abstracts Format

Title: (Concise and relevant to the conference theme)

Author(s):(Full Name, Affiliation, Contact Information)

Abstract:(250–300 words max, summarizing the research)

Keywords: (5–7 relevant keywords)

Call for Abstracts Format

(In MS Word, single-line spacing and Times New Roman font 12, single paragraph without subheading, indentation, or references. Should be an explicit summary of your presentation that states the problem, aim, methodology, and key results and conclusion). Kindly attach proof of the $5 payment (at 14 GHS or 1,1500NGN to $1) for abstract processing fee payment and publication in book of proceeding.















Publication Format:

All accepted abstract will be published in the Africa Recycling Initiative Conference Accra Proceedings.

Full papers of quality abstract will be invited for submission and consideration for publication in Topical Collection and Relevant Journal on Sustainable Waste Management in Africa and indexed in relevant academic databases as well as other partner journals on environmental sustainability and circular economy practices. (https://springer.com//journal/collection) .

Conference Registration Fee: $30 (46,500 NGN or 420 GHS) for students, and $100 (155,300NGN or 1,400 GHS) for Professionals
