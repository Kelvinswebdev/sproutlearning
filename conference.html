Conference Venue

10 – 11 April 2025 Accra, Ghana

After last year’s sold out event, we are doubling the capacity of openLCA.conf 2025. Our host location is ufaFabrik, a cultural and social centre in the south of Berlin. Spread across 18,500 sqm of beautiful grounds, this former movie lab is now home to 200 employees and 30 residents working on projects connecting social work, culture and ecology. The space boasts two large theaters for presentations, side rooms for workshops and meetings, a coffee/lounge area and a beautiful outside area. Directions: Metro line U6 to Ullsteinstraße.
